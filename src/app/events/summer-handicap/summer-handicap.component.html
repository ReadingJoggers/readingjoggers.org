How it works

You need to run a minimum of four races as your best four results will count towards your final points total.

If you have not taken part in the Handicap Series before or if you have not completed a race during the 2018 series you will have to complete five races. The extra race is needed to work out your handicap.

We will start you off in two minute intervals and ask that you are ready to start when your time comes around. Please do not ask to start in another 2 minute group as it will our life a lot easier and less likely that you are given the wrong time at the end.

Points are awarded as follows:

  • 1 point for first place
  • 2 points for second place
  • 3 points for third place
  • and so on...

Thus if there are 25 members taking part, the last place will score 25 points.

So the winner at the end of the season will be the member with the lowest number of points who has completed the required number of races, (4 if you ran last season or 5 if you did not).

Please note that MEMBERS ONLY will be awarded points, but guests will be welcome to take part, and they will be included in the results as guest runners.

PLEASE BE CAREFUL AND NOTE THAT YOU TAKE PART ENTIRELY AT YOUR OWN RISK

After the Handicap some of the members visit the Mansion House to replace some lost fluid.

Course

The course is accurately measured and comprises of one lap for the 2.5 miles and two laps for the 5 miles.

The course is also clearly marked with green spots and arrows.

The course uses the paths within Prospect Park and also the pavements on the outside of the park. You will have to cross two side roads off Leibenrood Road on each lap and the entrance to Prospect Park Hospital in Honey End Lane. You will also have to run on part of the road leading up to the Mansion House from the entrance to the park. On the two lap five mile course you will have to cross this road once, but there is a zebra crossing. There will be CAUTION RUNNERS signs on this road and near to the entrance of the park.
