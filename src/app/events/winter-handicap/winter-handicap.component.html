Winter Handicap

The 29th season (2019-2020) of the Reading Joggers Winter Handicap is now underway.

The course starts at the Pangbourne Rec Car Park (RG8 7BY), and then progresses to Sulham Woods where you do 'lap 2' of the old course. The return is by the same route. We meet at at 9:20am for 9:30am start. After a briefing, the first runners will be away by 9:40am.

The course is approximately 6.7 miles and is multiterrain. Please wear appropriate shoes and clothing if wet/cold.It is not suitable for spikes.

There is one road crossing on the new route - please take care

If you have not yet tried the winter handicaps it is a good run with a couple of hills and it could be wet, muddy and slippery. There are also tree roots so care is needed.

The course is marked with flour to show the route only. As it is new there will be printed copies of route handy for the first few.

As is common with a cross-country course, there may be hidden roots or slippery areas, so please take care. All participants run at their own risk.

After the run we will meet at tba Pangbourne cafe for hot drinks and cakes

This year there will be 6 Winter handicaps. Your best three finishes will count in the final placings. But if you have not taken part in the winter series before, did not run last season or complete the full distance you will need to run 4 of them. We need the extra race to establish a handicap for you. This is the reason your name may not appear in the list below.

Please note that only fully paid up members will be eligible for points but guests are welcome to take part in a maximum of 2 runs before being required to join the club and pay the membership fee.

Below are listed your name and handicap based on the results of the 2018/19 Winter Handicaps.

Those taking part will be sent off in small groups roughly related to your handicap time.

At the end, please make a note of your time as a back-up to the results.

This handicap will only run if we have sufficient volunteers to help mark the course (you can still run!) and/or run the event. Please let us know if you can help via email or facebook. We need 2 people to help mark per week for a ~8 am start.

Marker one will do the section from Pangbourne to Beech Road (approx 2 miles each way)

Marker two will do the Sulham Woods section (start from Sulham Woods car park) (approx 2 miles total)
