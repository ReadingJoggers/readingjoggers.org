Lansdowne Road / Grafton Road Circuit (Burlington Road)

We meet here for some training sessions. Check the events pages for details.

Directions

About 600-700 metres west of the Meadway Sports Centre you turn left off The Meadway into Halls Road.

Take the second right off Halls Road into Burlington Road.

At the end of Burlington Road is Lansdowne Road. Park safely on Lansdowne Road, but please do not block entrances to driveways.

You can also approach it from Langley Hill. Up Langley Hill to the roundabout and turn right into Halls Road.

Then take the second left into Burlington Road.

Address

Burlington Road, Reading, RG30 4QG

High Street (The Old A4), Theale

We meet here for some training sessions. Check the events pages for details.

Directions

You can go over the junction 12 motorway roundabout, towards the Theale bypass, and turn right at the next roundabout towards Theale. At the T-junction mini roundabout turn right and park up near the barrier.

If you decide to park at Beansheaf you can then jog to the session site as a warm up. You turn right into Sainsbury's and then left into Pincents Lane, passing Porsche Cars to your right and Dunelm to your left and head towards the footbridge over the motorway.

Address

High Street, Theale, Reading RG7 5AG
