Welcome to Reading Joggers

Just starting to run? Tired of running on your own? Preparing for a race? Whatever your ability, if you are looking for a friendly, local running club, we could be just what you are looking for. All ages and abilities are welcome.

Look around our website, or just come along to one of our regular club runs and training sessions where a warm welcome awaits you.
