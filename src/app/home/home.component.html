Welcome to Reading Joggers

Just starting to run? Tired of running on your own? Preparing for a race? Whatever your ability, if you are looking for a friendly, local jogging club, we could be just what you are looking for. All abilities and ages welcome.
Look around our website, or just come along to the Beansheaf Community Centre, Charrington Road, Calcot, Reading, RG31 7AW on a Tuesday or Thursday evening at 7:00 pm where a warm welcome awaits you.

Reading Joggers - Running since 1977

The club is affiliated to England Athletics.

Club meets every Tuesday and Thursday at 7:00 pm.

Discounts at local sports shops.

Club Handicap races organised throughout the year.
